Designed against the verified failure from today: a user saved the wrong day's cohort without noticing, because "what you're saving" rendered as a quiet def-list row. All variants promote the selected cohort to the top of the hierarchy and make the Static/Live consequence explicit. Mock data = the real scenario (user_recharge_daily, May 31 – Jun 6, one day-cohort picked).
