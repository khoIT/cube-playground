Why this placement

  • Zero new nav. CS agents already live here; money + support health sits right above the playbooks they action.

Why this placement

  • A real home for gateway / promo / identity / geo drill-downs — the analyst's first stop for monetization.
  • Hosts the reconciliation view (gateway-vs-ingame LTV) that the strip can't fit.
  • Each card is pin-to-dashboard ready and links straight into Catalog/Playground for the underlying query.
  • Cards reveal/hide per game readiness — cfm + jus today, more as games onboard.

Cost / risk

  • New top-level nav entry + route + empty/loading states = most build.
  • Another surface to keep on-design as the system evolves.
  • Overlaps the CS strip if both ship — pick one as the canonical home.
