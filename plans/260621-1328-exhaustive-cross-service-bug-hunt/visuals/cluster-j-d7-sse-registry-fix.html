Cross-service bug hunt · D7 · SSE stream registry

Cluster J — keeping the turn-stream registry honest under disconnect & abort

Chat turns stream over SSE and are mirrored into an in-memory registry so a refreshed client can replay and resume. Six bugs sat in that machinery: dead-socket writes, an abort that looked like an error, a leaked "running" slot that could wedge the concurrency cap, an over-eager alias sweep, and a replay offset that trusted client input.

⚠️ Design constraint that shaped the fixes: a client disconnect must not kill the turn — the registry deliberately keeps it alive for refresh-resume. So R24 guards the write, it does not abort.
