We run LiveOps for games at scale: millions of players, hundreds of thousands of events per second. The value of that data shows up two ways — acting on it in the moment (offers, rewards, win-back) and understanding it well enough to decide what's worth acting on. We already run an exploration engine and an AI authoring product in production, and a senior engineer is building the real-time decisioning core. What's missing is the person who makes it one platform: connecting the engines through a shared catalog, owning the seams, and automating the glue — like turning the onboarding of thousands of raw game-log streams from a manual chore into a pipeline.
