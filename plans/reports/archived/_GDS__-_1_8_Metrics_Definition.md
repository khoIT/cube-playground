# [GDS] - 1.8 Metrics Definition

**<u>Note:</u>**

- Users from test servers are excluded from all metrics.
- Time zone: All metrics are calculated from 00:00 to 23:59 (UTC+7).
- Day: Defined from 00:00 to 23:59 (UTC+7).
- Week (ISO): Defined from Monday to Sunday.
- Month: Defined from the 1st day to the last day of the month, calculated from 00:00 to 23:59 (UTC+7).
- Time format: DD/MM/YYYY (Ex: 01/01/2026, 30/12/2026)
- Active: A register, login, logout, or payment action recorded during the reporting period.
- Paying: Only successful payment transactions are included in all payment-related metrics
