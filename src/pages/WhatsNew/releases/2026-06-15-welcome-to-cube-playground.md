cube-playground is your analytics workspace over the game data lakehouse. Three
things to try first:

- **💬 Ask in natural language** — the [chat agent](/chat) answers questions like
  *"DAU for cfm_vn last 7 days"* and replies with real numbers, tables and charts.
  No SQL, no cube syntax.
- **🔍 Explore in the playground** — [build a query](/build) by picking measures,
  dimensions and filters, then see live results from Trino in seconds and switch
  between table and chart views.
- **🎯 Build segments** — turn rules into reusable [cohorts](/segments)
  (e.g. *recently-active payers*), refreshed nightly and ready for Care, exports
  and analysis.

New features land here in **What's New** — the bell up top badges anything you
haven't read yet.
