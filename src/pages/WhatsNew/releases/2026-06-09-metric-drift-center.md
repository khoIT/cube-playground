Find and fix **broken metric references** in one place.

- Root-cause grouping clusters every metric that breaks on the same missing member.
- One-click **repoint-ref** moves a metric to a live `/meta` member from a searchable picker.
- **Mark-N/A** retires references whose upstream member is gone for good.

Head to the Drift Center to clear the current backlog.
